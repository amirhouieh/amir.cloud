Amir Houieh



I am Amir Houieh and I am a creative technologist and co-founder of SUSLIB. I explore, research, experiment and create where technology and design intersect. I design the design process. I formulate the design process into code and transform data into interactive narratives/experiences (digital/physical). While I have always had a keen passion towards tech and it's implementation, my focus is more on the application of technology where I believe there is still lots of room for improvements. Machine learning, A.R and web technologies are the most tech stack which I use in my projects.

Since July 2016 (when I graduated from Royal Academy of Art, The Hague - KABK), I have been giving lectures, workshops and also teach in the field of design and creative coding and technologies. As of September 2017, I have been teaching at the Graphic Design department at Artez, University of Arts in Arnhem, The Netherlands.
