Amir Houieh



Amir Houieh is media designer, creative coder, and instructor of these disciplines. I explore alternative ways in which digital information has shaped our daily life. I design the design process. I have been developing a consciousness towards the tools and the medium I choose for my design. Programming has become a powerful tool for me and digital media an endless medium. I am dedicated in research and developing new methodologies, applications and platforms in digital publishing; how we consume and produce the information in digital environments.
Since Oct 2018, I am trying to establish my own little zone (studio/lab?) called XTXT, where I focus on self initiative projects and experiments. As September 2017, I have been teaching at Graphic Design department at Artez, University of Arts in Arnhem, The Netherlands. The course titles Digital Media in which we focus on the intersection of technology and programming and design. And as February 2019 I start teaching an elective course name Coding Atelier at Fine Art department of, Royal Academy of Art (KABK).
